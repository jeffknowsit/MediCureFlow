Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Log in to your account, search for a specialist by name or department, select a time slot that works for you, and confirm your booking.

Can I cancel an appointment?

Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before the scheduled time via your dashboard for a full refund of any prepaid fees.

Is my medical data safe?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption and comply with all international health data privacy standards (HIPAA/GDPR).

How do I join as a medical professional?

Check the "Join as Doctor" link in the footer to submit your credentials and start the verification process.

Still need help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or technical issues.
